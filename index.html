Who We Are

Classik Chemical was started as a small scale company in May 1990 with a primary aim to provide a quality product at competitive price with high customer satisfaction. Due to steady growth Classik Chemical established their chemical factory in Erode, Tamilnadu, India. We were initially focusing on manufacturing textile auxiliary chemicals that meets the local textile market.

We expanded into Dyes and Chemicals with our own reputable brands to cater for ever increasing demands of our customers and textile market.

Our premium products, timely delivery and customer friendly service have made as a leading textile manufacturing house in Tamil Nadu.

Support

We have dedicated team of technicians who continue to work on producing a new product and formulas to support the need of our diversified customer base. Our proactive attitude has helped us to retain our customer confidence and continue to be their first preferred supplier which has helped us to be one of pioneer of the textile chemical field for the past two decades.

Quality

Over the last two decades we have adopted various industry leading manufacturing technique that aligns with “Export Quality Norms”. Commitment to quality and customer support remains a cornerstone of our business. This has helped us differentiate us from the other manufacturers.
